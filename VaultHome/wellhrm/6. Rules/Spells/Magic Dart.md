#### Magic Dart #cantrip

- **Spell Level:** Magic-User #cantrip 
- **Range:**  60ft
- **Duration:**  Immediate

A magical dart flies where the caster directs, with a range of 60 feet. It hits automatically... 90% of the time. Roll a d10, anything other than a 1 hits and does 1d3 damage.
