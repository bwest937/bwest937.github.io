### Charisma

Characters with high Charisma get a bonus on reaction and morale rolls and can hire more specialists.

#### Table A3 Charisma
| **CHA Score** | **Hire Morale** | **Max Specialists** |
|:-----------------:|:---------------:|:-------------------:|
|         3         |        1        |         -4          |
|        4-5        |        2        |         -2          |
|        6-8        |        3        |         -1          |
|       9-12        |        4        |          0          |
|       13-15       |        5        |         +1          |
|       16-17       |        6        |         +2          |
|        18         |        7        |         +4          |
