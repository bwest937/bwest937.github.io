### Charisma

Characters with high Charisma get a bonus on reaction rolls and their hired help is more loyal.

#### Table A3 Charisma
| CHA Score | Max Retainers | Loyalty |
|:---------:|:-------------:| ------- |
|     3     |       1       | 4       |
|    4-5    |       2       | 5       |
|    6-8    |       3       | 6       |
|   9-12    |       4       | 7       |
|   13-15   |       5       | 8       |
|   16-17   |       6       | 9       |
|    18     |       7       | 10      |
